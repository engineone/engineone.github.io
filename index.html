Why is this important?



We like to think that we are not the only ones feeling like being a developer is becoming harder and harder every year!

Just less than a decade ago you needed just a handful of skills to be a successful software developer. Think about the number of skills you need to put in your resume in 2017 for it to look attractive. Even though you don't need them on daily basis, they are a good indication of the cognitive load that developers have to cope under.

Through your input and advancements in AI, we believe we can automate enough processes in software development to lessen this load and make software development great again!

How can you help?


  • 1  Send us your email address above

    We promise that we will not share your email address with anyone. We will only use it to communicate information / updates about EngineOne that we believe might be of interest to you. Every email we send to you will feature an unsubscribe link that you can use should you wish to do so.

  • 2  Appointment

    We will reach out to you to make an appointment where we can have the conversation.

  • 3  Interview

    The interview is designed to take about 20 to 30 minutes.
    The interview will be split up into three sections:
    • Short questions about how you work and your experiences
    • A youTube demo
    • Quick rating and feedback on demo

  • 4  Recording

    We prefer the interviews to be recorded so that we can spend time talking instead of typing. You can opt not to have the recording done. This recording can be made available to you at any point.

    Our tool of choice of choice is zoom but can use any tool that you are comfortable with

  • 5  Keeping in touch

    We value your feedback and expertise and would like to keep in contact with you. So we will keep you up-to-date with major developments and might occasionally ask some follow up questions.
