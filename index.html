Why is this important?



We like to think that we are not the only ones feeling like being a developer is becoming harder and harder every year!

Even though you don't need them on daily basis, they are a good indication of the cognitive load that developers have to cope under.

Through your input and advancements in AI, we believe we can automate enough processes in software development to lessen this load and make software development great again!
